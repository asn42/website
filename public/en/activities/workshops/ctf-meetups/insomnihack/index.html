Insomni'Hack Teaser CTF

Let's make a good start this year, with a proposition from Association Sans Nom to take a stab at computer security with an challenge online : the Teaser CTF of Insomni'Hack, which will start at 10:00 on Saturday, January 18, and last 24 hours..

The idea is to get together in the cluster to help each other and share a good time. You can show up and leave when you want, although it's obviously more enjoyable to start at the same time.

The only prerequisite is a craving for brain-teasers and some curiosity. If it works for you, don't hesitate to join us in the first rows, right of E2.
